Beers

Red Fox

  • Amber Ale
  • 6% ABV
  • Bold, refreshing, introspective.
  • This ale loves to quench your thirst and long walks on the beach.
  • Goes great with: fresh fish, carbohydrates

Lincoln Lager

  • German-style lager
  • 5% ABV
  • Crisp, light, inquisitive
  • This Dusseldorf-inspired lager is quadruple distilled at near-freezing temperatures for a clean, efficient taste.
  • Goes great with: pears and other thin-skinned fruit

State St. Stout

  • English stout
  • 9% ABV
  • Dark and hearty
  • Bold, bitter, and always looking for a brawl, this hearty English stout is sure to bring your tastebuds to a rousing standing ovation.
  • Goes great with: salad
